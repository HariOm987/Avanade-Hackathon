Empowering talent, embracing diversity
Skills above stereotypes

A revolutionary platform that aims to reduce gender bias and discrimination in the hiring process and workplace. Our platform ensures fair opportunities for all by hiding the gender and name of the applicant from recruiters, encouraging gender-neutral language in job postings, and rewarding recruiters for demonstrating gender-neutral behavior.

Blind Screening

Our platform ensures that job applicants' personal information, such as name and gender, is concealed from recruiters until the screening process is complete, promoting unbiased hiring practices and creating equal opportunities for candidates from all backgrounds.

Gender-neutral job descriptions

Our platform uses natural language processing to identify and remove gendered language from job descriptions. By doing so, we help employers create a more welcoming and inclusive work environment, leading to better hiring outcomes and a more diverse workplace.

Point system for recruiters

Recruiters can earn points by submitting gender-neutral paragraphs to increase their credibility. Additionally, a minimum point requirement ensures only non-biased recruiters can post jobs. Points can also be purchased, providing flexibility and ease of use for all recruiters.

Community-driven feedback

The community-driven feedback feature allows job applicants to provide feedback on their application experience, enabling the system to identify areas of bias or any issues that may need to be addressed. This feedback is then used to improve the application process and further reduce any potential gender bias.
